# Code of conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community. Be constructive,
assume good faith, welcome different experience levels, and focus criticism on
the work rather than the person.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks,
- sexualized language or unwanted attention,
- publishing another person's private information,
- deliberately disrupting project discussions,
- sharing private, proprietary, or sensitive artifacts in public project
  spaces.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, and other
project spaces, and when someone publicly represents the project.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns to the maintainer through the
[maintainer's GitHub profile](https://github.com/xodnr927-byte). Do not include
private or sensitive details in a public issue. Repository maintainers may
edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban
participants whose behavior is inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

Reports will be reviewed as promptly and confidentially as reasonably
possible. Enforcement decisions will consider the context, severity, and
pattern of the behavior.
